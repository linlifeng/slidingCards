A book I can read myself - book 1

Lawrence was drawing pictures and he ran out of paper

"I will just draw on the wall then!" said Lawrence.

"Lawrence!!" Mom saw this and was very mad.

Lawrence is super scared.

He doesn't know what to do.

He does not like it when mom gets mad.

Lawrence feels the world is chasing him.

He wants to run away and hide.

So mom will not find him.

Mom went off to find Lawrence.

She climbed over mountains of doodles.

However, Mom can't find Lawrence anywhere.

She is very sad and tired.

She missed Lawrence so much.

Mom met a monster that looks like Lawrence, but with paint brushes as his face and paint bucket as his body.

She told the monster that she missed Lawrence.

Lawrence came crawling out from under the rug.

Mom is not mad anymore.

I know I can be messy sometimes.

But mom loves me no matter what.
